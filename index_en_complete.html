🍳 Privacy Policy — Batchcook

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Batchcook ("the App") is designed to respect your privacy. This page explains what data is processed and how.

Data We Collect

No personal data is collected, transmitted, or stored on our servers.

The App does not require a user account or registration and does not ask for your name, email address, or location.

Data Stored on Your Device

The information you enter (number of people, cooking preferences, budget, cooking time, weekly meal plan, and shopping list) is stored only locally on your device. It never leaves your device and is deleted if you uninstall the App.

Third-Party Services

  • Recipe images: Loaded from Wikimedia Commons, TheMealDB, and barbecue-co.com. Only standard technical request information (IP address and device type) is transmitted. None of your entered data is sent. Images are cached on your device after the first download.
  • Real prices: Using the Refresh Prices button queries the public Open Prices API from Open Food Facts for average food prices. No personal information, meal plans, or shopping lists are transmitted.

The App does not use advertising, analytics, or tracking services.

Permissions

Calendar: If you use the Add to Calendar feature, the App requests calendar access only to create your planned meal events locally. It does not read existing events or transmit calendar data.

No other sensitive permissions are requested (camera, contacts, or location).

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect children's data.

Your Rights

Since no personal data is collected remotely, there is nothing for us to export or delete. You can remove all data by uninstalling the App.
